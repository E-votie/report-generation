								WHO IS A CITIZEN|ELECTOR|VOTER
							

								Who is a Citizen?
							

								Person who is entitled to enjoy all the legal rights and privileges granted by a state to the people comprising is constituency, and is obligated to obey its laws and  to 
								fulfill his or duties as called upon. Also called a national.
							

								Who is an Elector?
							



											1
										

											A person who has the right to vote at an election.

										



											2
										

											A person entitled to participate in an election.
										


Every one above the age of 18 can be called as an Elector
  

  

								Who is a Voter?
					    

								A Voter is the person who is registered in the Electoral Register, 
								who has an identity document and who is eligible to participate at an election.
